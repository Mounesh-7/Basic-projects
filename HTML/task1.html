-History of Zoho:

Zoho Corporation is an Indian multinational technology company that makes computer software and web-based business tools. It is best known for the online office suite offering Zoho Office Suite. The company was founded in 1996 by Sridhar Vembu and Tony Thomas has a presence in seven locations with head quarters in Chennai, Tamil Nadu,India, and corporate headquaters outside of Austin in Del Valle, Texas.
